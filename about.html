We Are Bioworld, We Want To Bioworld With Our Eco-Friendly Products

Our mission is to produce and provide the highest quality bagasse tableware manufactured with the help of modern equipment and the latest technology so we can say goodbye to poisonous one-time-use plastic.

From 2016

Our Story

Bioworld has been the leading bagasse tableware exporter for the last 9 years, and now we have expanded our wings to the manufacturing unit too. We plan to manufacture around 1,00,00,000 Kgs of 100% eco-friendly bagasse products for the Indian and overseas markets.

Speaking of worldwide presence, we have offices in three different nations. Our passion to help the environment and make the world a better world for coming generations is what drives our team to deliver their best.

Made In India

We wholeheartedly support the Indian PM Shri Narendra Modi's initiative called Make in India. Our bagasse products are manufactured in the Morbi district of Gujarat with the latest technologies and highly qualified staff.

Swachh Bharat

We endorse the Indian government's Swachh Bharat mission and encourage the Indian population to do the same. We encourage the Indian audience to reduce the use of one-time-use plastic which is responsible for a big part of environmental pollution.
